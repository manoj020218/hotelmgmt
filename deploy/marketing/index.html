From Scan to Served — in 60 Seconds

One seamless loop connects your guest, waiter, and kitchen with zero friction.

Features

Everything Your Hotel Needs

Built for real hospitality operations — multi-tenant, real-time, and mobile-first.

📋

Digital Menu Management

Add, edit, and categorise menu items with images and pricing from the admin panel. Changes reflect instantly for guests.

⚡

Real-Time Order Flow

WebSocket-powered live updates across all screens — admin, waiter, and KDS — with no polling or refreshing needed.

📱

Android Apps (KDS & Waiter)

Native Android APKs for Kitchen Display System and Waiter. Runs offline-capable with Firebase Cloud Messaging push alerts.

💳

UPI QR Payments

Attach your UPI QR to each hotel. Guests pay at the table; admin marks payment received or disputed in one tap.

📊

Analytics Dashboard

Track daily revenue, top items, order volumes, and session data. Filter by day, week, or month with chart exports.

🏨

Multi-Hotel / Multi-Tenant

One platform, multiple hotels. Each property has isolated data, its own menu, QR codes, and staff accounts.

🔒

Role-Based Access

Admin, Waiter, and KDS roles. Each sees only what they need. JWT-secured with refresh token rotation.

🖨️

Printable QR Codes

Generate and download QR codes for every table. Stick them on table tents, frames, or laminated cards.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

HotelQR ("we", "our") is operated by Jenix India. We take privacy seriously.

  • Data Collected: Guest orders (table number, items, session ID), hotel staff accounts (name, email, hashed password), and payment status flags. No credit card data is stored.
  • How Used: Order data is used solely to fulfil the order and display analytics to the hotel admin. We do not sell or share data with third parties.
  • Push Notifications: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) is used to send order alerts to the Waiter and KDS apps. FCM tokens are stored per-device and deleted on logout.
  • Cookies: The admin PWA uses localStorage for JWT tokens. No third-party tracking cookies are used.
  • Data Retention: Order data is retained as long as your hotel account is active. You may request deletion at any time.
  • Contact: For privacy concerns, email jenixindia@gmail.com.
